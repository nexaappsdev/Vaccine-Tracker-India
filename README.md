# Vaccine Tracker India 💉

> Your child's vaccination companion — offline, private, and built for Indian parents.

Never miss a vaccine again. Vaccine Tracker India helps you track every dose on 
India's National Immunization Schedule (NIS), get timely reminders, and keep 
your child's health records organised — all without an internet connection or account.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📷 Scan & Import
- Scan your **existing paper vaccine cards** using your phone camera
- Import records instantly — no manual entry needed
- Free unlimited scans for your first child *(Pro for additional children)*

### 🤖 AI Chat
- Ask vaccine questions and get instant answers
- Powered by Claude AI — understands Indian vaccine context
- Available to **Pro users**

### 📅 Smart Vaccine Schedule
- Pre-loaded with India's official **National Immunization Schedule (NIS)**
- Source: Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India
- Covers **BCG, OPV, IPV, Hepatitis B, Pentavalent, Rotavirus, PCV, MR, 
  Vitamin A, DPT, Td, JE** — from birth to 16 years
- Automatically calculates due dates based on your child's date of birth

### 👶 Multiple Children
- Add and manage **multiple children** in one app
- Each child gets their own personalised vaccine timeline
- Track progress independently for each child

### 🔔 Smart Reminders
- Get notified **1, 3, 7, or 14 days before** a vaccine is due
- Daily reminders for **overdue vaccines** so nothing slips through
- Customisable — turn on only what works for you

### 📄 Export as PDF
- Generate a **clean, shareable PDF** of your child's vaccination record
- Perfect for school admissions, travel, and doctor visits
- No more hunting for that paper card

### 🔒 100% Private & Offline
- **No account required** — ever
- All data stays on your device
- Nothing is uploaded, synced, or shared
- Supports Android's Google device backup (optional, controlled by you)

### 🎨 Clean, Modern Design
- Light & dark mode
- Simple timeline view — see what's done, what's due, what's overdue at a glance
- Works on Android 8.0 and above
